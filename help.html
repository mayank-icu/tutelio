About Tutelio Platform

Tutelio is an AI-powered educational platform designed to provide high-quality learning experiences for students up to class 10. Our mission is to make education more accessible and effective, especially for those who may not have access to traditional schooling.

The platform offers interactive learning experiences with AI-generated explanations, exercises, and personalized learning recommendations. Students can ask questions and receive instant assistance in various subjects.

Tutelio's minimal and user-friendly interface ensures a distraction-free learning environment that makes education enjoyable and effective.

Key Benefits:
  • Personalized learning paths tailored to individual strengths and weaknesses
  • Interactive explanations that adapt to different learning styles
  • Real-time feedback on answers and solutions
  • Gamified learning elements to increase engagement
  • Accessible on all devices with internet connectivity

Learn more about our educational philosophy and research-backed approach.

Getting Started with Tutelio

Follow these steps to begin your learning journey with Tutelio:

  1. Create an account by providing basic information
  2. Select your grade level and preferred subjects
  3. Complete the initial assessment to personalize your learning path
  4. Explore the dashboard and available learning resources

Quick Tips:

  • Be specific when asking questions to get the most accurate help

  • Use the "Explain Again" feature if you need concepts explained differently
  • Save important lessons and explanations to your personal library

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects does Tutelio cover?

Tutelio currently covers Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies (History, Geography, Civics), English (Grammar, Literature, Comprehension), and basic programming (Scratch, Python) for students up to class 10. We regularly update our content based on the latest curriculum standards.

How does the AI tutoring work?

Our AI analyzes your questions and learning patterns to provide personalized explanations, examples, and practice problems tailored to your learning style and needs. The more you interact with Tutelio, the better it adapts to your unique learning requirements, providing increasingly relevant and helpful guidance.

Is Tutelio available on mobile devices?

Yes, Tutelio is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and computers, allowing you to learn anytime, anywhere. We also offer dedicated apps for iOS and Android devices with additional offline learning capabilities.

Can parents track their child's progress?

Yes, parents can create a linked account to monitor learning progress, time spent, and areas of improvement for their children. Our parent dashboard provides insights into subjects studied, concept mastery, and learning trends.

Is internet connectivity required at all times?

While Tutelio works best with internet connectivity, our mobile apps allow you to download lessons for offline access. Certain interactive features may be limited without an internet connection, but core learning materials remain available.

How is Tutelio different from traditional tutoring?

Unlike traditional tutoring with fixed schedules, Tutelio provides 24/7 access to learning resources. Our AI can explain concepts in multiple ways, adapting to your learning style. We also offer features like instant feedback, adaptive practice, and personalized learning paths not available in conventional tutoring.

Can Tutelio help with homework and exam preparation?

Absolutely! You can upload homework problems to receive step-by-step guidance (not just answers). For exam preparation, Tutelio provides topic reviews, practice tests, and targeted exercises based on your areas of weakness.

How secure is my child's data on Tutelio?

We take privacy seriously. Tutelio is COPPA-compliant and we never share student data with third parties. All learning interactions are encrypted, and parents have full visibility into their child's account. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

Have more questions? Check our comprehensive FAQ section or contact our support team.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

The AI tutor isn't understanding my questions

Try rephrasing your question with more details or break it into smaller parts. Use subject-specific terminology for better results. Including keywords related to the topic helps the AI provide more accurate responses.

I can't access my saved lessons

Check your internet connection and refresh the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try logging in from a different device. Make sure you're using the latest version of our app if accessing through mobile.

My progress isn't being saved

Make sure you're logged in and complete each lesson module fully. Click the "Save Progress" button explicitly if you need to exit before completion. For mobile users, ensure you have sufficient storage space available.

The interactive exercises aren't loading

This usually happens due to browser compatibility issues. Try using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari with the latest updates. Disable any ad-blockers or browser extensions that might interfere with the interactive elements.

I forgot my password

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support for assistance.

The voice recognition feature isn't working

Make sure you've granted microphone access permissions to Tutelio. Speak clearly and in a quiet environment. This feature works best on Chrome browsers and may have limited functionality on some mobile devices.

For more assistance, visit our Help Center or Technical Support page.
