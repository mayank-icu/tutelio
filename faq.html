Frequently Asked Questions

Tutelio is an AI-powered educational platform designed to provide high-quality learning experiences for students up to class 10. Our platform uses artificial intelligence to offer personalized explanations, interactive exercises, and learning recommendations tailored to each student's needs and learning pace. Students can access lessons, ask questions, and receive instant feedback through our user-friendly interface.

Currently, Tutelio supports all major subjects for students up to class 10, including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and more. Our curriculum is aligned with standard educational frameworks to ensure comprehensive coverage. We're actively working on expanding our offerings to include higher classes and additional specialized subjects in the near future.

Tutelio's AI system analyzes each student's learning patterns, strengths, and areas for improvement to provide personalized education. Students can ask subject-related questions at any time and receive instant, accurate responses. The AI adapts its teaching approach based on how you learn best, breaking down complex concepts into simpler explanations when needed and providing more challenging content as you progress.

Yes, Tutelio is fully responsive and works seamlessly across all devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Our platform is designed with a minimal, distraction-free interface that adapts to different screen sizes, ensuring students can access quality education anytime, anywhere, on any device with internet connectivity.

Unlike traditional online courses that follow a rigid structure, Tutelio provides a truly interactive learning experience that adapts to each student's needs. Our AI doesn't just present information—it engages with students, answers specific questions, identifies knowledge gaps, and adjusts content difficulty in real-time. This creates a personalized learning journey more similar to having a dedicated tutor than watching pre-recorded videos or reading static content.

Tutelio offers various subscription plans to suit different needs and budgets. We believe in making quality education accessible, so we provide a free basic tier with limited features, allowing students to experience our platform before committing. Our premium plans offer full access to all subjects, unlimited AI assistance, and additional features at competitive rates. Please visit our pricing page for current subscription details and special educational discounts.

Currently, Tutelio focuses on supplementing regular education rather than providing formal certifications. However, as part of our growth plan, we're developing certification programs to acknowledge student achievements and progress. These upcoming certifications will validate learning milestones and potentially serve as recognized credentials. We'll announce these features as they become available.

Currently, Tutelio is designed to complement traditional education by providing additional support, practice, and personalized learning experiences. While our platform offers comprehensive content coverage, it's not yet a complete replacement for school education. However, we're working towards developing more comprehensive programs that could potentially serve as alternative education options in the future, especially for students who don't have access to quality traditional schooling.

We maintain high standards for educational content through a rigorous multi-step verification process. Our curriculum is developed by experienced educators and subject matter experts, then reviewed for accuracy, clarity, and alignment with educational standards. Our AI systems are regularly updated with the latest educational content and trained to provide accurate information. We also welcome user feedback to continuously improve our content quality.

While most of Tutelio's interactive features require internet connectivity to access our AI capabilities, we understand not all students have consistent access. That's why we offer downloadable lesson materials and practice exercises that can be used offline. Once back online, students can sync their progress and continue with the full interactive experience. We're continuously working to improve our offline functionality.
